My Monthly Budget.

Hello and welcome to My Monthly Budget! Here you can quickly type in your incomes and costs to have an overview of your budget, and how much you can save for next month! Let's start by chosing (+) for income or (-) for cost, then write which kind of income/cost and then write in how much. Press "Let's add it!" or ENTER to add to your budget! Voilá!
